## How Booty is Calculated

Booty = High Alchemy Value × Quantity

For example:
- Adamant 2h sword: 3,120 GP high alch × 1 = 3,120 booty
- 100 Iron ore: 40 GP high alch × 100 = 4,000 booty

This encourages players to value items beyond just their GE price and provides a consistent progression system tied to actual game mechanics.
